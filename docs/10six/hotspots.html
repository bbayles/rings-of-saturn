14. Migrating Hotspots

You will notice that hotspots move out from under your Wells. This is part of the volatile nature of Transium, and you will have to learn to keep an eye on your Transium supply and your Wells to make sure hotspots have not migrated. When they do, right click the closest Well and choose "Relocate," which will allow you to rebuild that well in the new location.

All buildings may be moved in this fashion, but it does cost more Transium than a normal build operation.

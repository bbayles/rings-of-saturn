17. Communication Groups

You may form groups with other players using the Communication Interface. Click on   "Entire Camp" at the left portion of the chat window. This reveals a pop-up menu; select "Edit Groups," which brings up the Communication Group Interface.

Here you may create groups, accept invitations to join groups, and invite others to join your group. Groups are only formed with members of the same corpororation. You can also form unique groups, called cadres, which have special rankings and stature on Visitor. You can also invite others to join your Mutual Defense Network. You establish MDN relationships with other players, pledging to come to each other's aid. If you have an MDN, you can then use the Alert MDN button (red one next to teleport back), which sends an automatic message to any MDN members online.

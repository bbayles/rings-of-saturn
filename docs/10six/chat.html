16. Chat

You may speak to other players in the camp you are in by simply hitting "Enter," typing into the chat area, and "Enter" to send. 10Six has a full chat system, but here are some of the most important commands.

%tell <playername> <message> -- whispers to that player within a camp

%yell <playername> <message> -- whispers to that player anywhere in the game (TU cost)

%who <playername> -- gets info on that player

%who <campnumber> -- gets info on that camp

%find <campnumber> -- only works within World View; zooms into that camp

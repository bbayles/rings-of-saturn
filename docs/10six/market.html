10. Corporate Market

Go into the Nerve Center. Press the Corp Market button. Here you may exchange Transium Units and Visitor Dollars, but the corporation takes a percentage of each Transfer. Click "Sell TUs" and click in the text entry box. Type in 1500 and click "Exchange." 1500 TUs will be taken out of your Wells and Silos and V$ will be deposited in your account (noted next to your avatar portrait).

Now that you have cash, click on the Purchase button in the Jitter Pack Purchase area. This deducts 2500 of those V$ and gives you 10 new random Jitters in your backpack. If you do not have room in your backpack you will be told and the transaction is cancelled. So if you want to try to get new items, then use your surplus Transium to purchase new Jitters.

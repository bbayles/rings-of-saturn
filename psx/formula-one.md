This is a patch for the *Demo One (Version 4)* preview CD that modifies its
demo of *Formula 1*. You can normally play on one track; this patch enables
access to three more.

The demo files are dated 1996-07-31, a little more than two weeks before the final
build's files (which are from 1996-08-11).

The Releases page on this repository has an XDelta patch that can be applied with [Delta Patcher](https://github.com/marco-calautti/DeltaPatcher/releases/tag/v3.1.5):
* __Original file__: `Demo One (Version 4) (Europe) (Track 01).bin`
* __XDelta patch__: `formula-one-demo.xdelta`

The Releases page on this repository has an XDelta patch for *Official U.S. PlayStation Magazine Demo Disc 36* that extends
the preview of *Dave Mirra Freestyle BMX*.

Normally this preview limits you to one type of gameplay, but the patch allows you to
select among several different ones.

The demo's files are from is from 2000-05-18, 3 months before the final U.S. version.

Apply the patch with [Delta Patcher](https://github.com/marco-calautti/DeltaPatcher/releases/tag/v3.1.5):
* __Original file__: `Official U.S. PlayStation Magazine Demo Disc 36 (USA) (Track 1).bin`
* __XDelta patch__: `dave-mirra-demo.xdelta`

After booting the demo disc, select *Dave Mirra Freestyle BMX* from the list of demos on the main menu.

The releases page for this repository has a patch for the preview of *Disruptor* on the "Green" *PlayStation Picks*
demo disc. That demo normally lets you play three stages, but the disc has data for
three more.

Apply the patch with [Delta Patcher](https://github.com/marco-calautti/DeltaPatcher/releases/tag/v3.1.5):
* __Original file__: `PlayStation Picks (USA) (SCUS-94960) (Track 1).bin`
* __XDelta patch__: `disruptor-demo.xdelta`

Select the *Disruptor* demo from the main menu (first row, second column) to play:
* The three additional stages are available from the "Preview Additional Missions" menu.
* Press Select to enter cheats. L1+R1 toggles invincibility; L2+R2 refills ammo and health.

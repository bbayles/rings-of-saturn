The releases page for this repository contains patches for the preview versions of *Tales of Destiny* for PlayStation
that enable debug features and allow for exploration beyond the normal demo limits.

Apply them with [Delta Patcher](https://github.com/marco-calautti/DeltaPatcher/releases/tag/v3.1.5).

For the 1997-07-31 build:
* __Original file__: `Tales of Destiny (Japan) (Preview Edition by Namco) (Track 1).bin`
* __XDelta patch__: `tod-namco-demo.xdelta`

For the 1997-10-09 build:
* __Original file__: `Tales of Destiny (Japan) (Preview Edition by Namco) (Famitsuu Version) (Track 1).bin`
* __XDelta patch__: `tod-famitsu-demo.cue`

A writeup with more details is available at [Rings of Saturn](https://32bits.substack.com/p/bf4e7494-f911-432c-b577-c0c3fe6bdd6e).
